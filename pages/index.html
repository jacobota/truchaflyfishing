The Trucha Narrative

WELCOME,

TRUCHA Fly Fishing Guide Service specializes in guided fly fishing (catch and release) on Crowley Lake, and surrounding Eastern Sierra waters. Crowley Lake offers some of the best Stillwater fly fishing for trout (aka Trucha in Spanish) this side of the southwest, and perhaps in the entire country. If you enjoy fishing, the outdoors, and our California Eastern Sierras, then you owe it to yourself to fly fish on Crowley Lake for “Trucha” that are healthy, athletic, and amazingly strong fighting fish!

-Trucha Fly Fishing Guide, David Carranza

About Your Guide

● Fishing Guide David Carranza, has been fishing the Eastern Sierra since early childhood, and Crowley Lake since 1998. David resides on Crowley Lake full-time from May to the end of October, and fishes the lake extensively.

● David's belief, "Fly fishing is not as difficult as it may appear! I will introduce you to the mechanics of the fly fishing rod and reel, and guide you along to enjoy a fun and memorable fly fishing experience”. Battling a Crowley Lake fish on a fly rod is exciting, addicting, and a complete adrenaline rush! Come and experience the passion!

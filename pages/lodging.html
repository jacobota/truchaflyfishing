LODGING

Planning Your Trip

Trucha Fly Fishing Guide Service (TFFGS) has experienced many years of personal lodging and/or camping in the Eastern Sierra. I endorse and highly recommend the following three links and facilities to assist you in planning your stay and fishing trip while in the Eastern Sierra.

Mono County Lodging Website

● Mono County has lodging options to suit your every need.

● Whether you are looking for a luxury mountain resort, rustic cabin retreat, or a family friendly campground.

● Search by bed and breakfasts, cabins and chalets, campgrounds, condos, hotels & motels, lodges & inns, resorts, pet friendly accommodations and RV Parks.

Crowley Lake Fish Camp

● There are 3 cabins located on the camp which are just awesome for yourself, family, and/or friends.

● The cabins have a full bedroom, loft with two full size beds, couch, cable TV, full kitchen and utensils, linens, refrigerator, propane BBQ, and a fire pit.

● The cabins are very comfortable, and offer spectacular views of the lake and the surrounding granite mountains.

● There are full hook-up and dry camping sites available within the Fish Camp.

● Click below for more information:

McGee Creek RV Park

● McGee Creek flows from the mountain peaks down the middle of this camping facility and is always stocked with trout during the camping and trout season.

● The camp has full RV hook up sites and dry camping sites that sit on grassy areas which is great for tent campers.

● McGee Creek RV has a well maintained bathroom and shower facility separately for men and women.

● There are three beautiful trout ponds on the site which are also open for publicfishing on a fee payment scale.

● And just across the Highway 395 from Crowley Lake.
